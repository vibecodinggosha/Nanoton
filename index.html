01 ⚛️
WHAT IS NANOTON?

1 nanoton = 10⁻⁹ TON. It is the smallest indivisible unit of Toncoin — the base denomination of The Open Network, just like Wei is to Ethereum. Every single fee, gas cost, and storage payment on TON is calculated and settled in nanotons.

02 ⚡
THE FUEL OF TON

All transaction fees on TON — compute gas, message forwarding, storage rent — are denominated in nanotons under the hood. 1 TON = 1,000,000,000 nanoton. Every smart contract, every wallet, every NFT on TON runs on nanoton-level precision.

03 🔬
PRECISION BY DESIGN

TON fees are among the lowest in crypto — often just a few thousand nanotons per transaction. NANOTON embodies that ethos: ultra-precise, infinitely small, yet the foundation that keeps the entire network running.

04 🏗️
BUILT INTO THE PROTOCOL

The nanoton unit is hardcoded into TON's core architecture. Gas prices, storage fees, forward fees and IHR fees are all computed in nanotons by the TVM (TON Virtual Machine) at the lowest level of the blockchain.

05 📐
THE MATH

1 TON  = 1,000,000,000 nTON
1 nTON = 0.000000001 TON
1 nTON = 10-9 TON
NANOTON is that unit — tokenized.
